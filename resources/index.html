resources

a summary of source code and databases for the works that I have contributed to.

source code

video coding

  • ViSTRA [TCSVT2018/SPIC2021, code]: video compression based on spatial resolution and effective bit depth adaptation
  • CNN-PP [MM2020, code]: video compression with CNN-based post processing
  • MFRNet [JSTSP2020, code]: a new CNN architecture for post-processing and in-loop filtering
  • CVE-GAN [SPIC2024, code]: a perceptually-inspired GAN for compressed video enhancement
  • HiNeRV [NeurIPS2023, code]: video compression with hierarchical encoding-based neural representation
  • MV-HiNeRV [PCS2024, code]: Immersive Video Compression using Implicit Neural Representations
  • NVRC [NeurIPS2024, code]: Neural Video Representation Compression

quality models

  • FRQM [ICIP2017, code]: a frame rate dependent video quality metric
  • PVM [TCSVT2015, [code](/assets/zips/PVM_code.zip]: a perception-based video metric
  • FloLPIPS [PCS2022, code]: A Bespoke Video Quality Metric for Frame Interpoation
  • RankDVQA [WACV2024, code]: Deep VQA based on a Novel Hybrid Training Methodology
  • RMT-BVQA [ECCVW2024, code]: Recurrent Memory Transformer-based Blind Video Quality Assessment for Enhanced Video Content
  • MVAD [WACV2025, code]: A Multiple Visual Artifact Detector for Video Streaming

other image processing methods

  • ST-MFNet [CVPR2022, code]: Spatio-Temporal Multi-Flow Network for Video Frame Interpolation
  • ST-MFNet Mini [ICIP2023, code]: the compact model of ST-MFNet
  • Deformable Convolution based VFI [ICIP2022, code]: a new deformable convolution based VFI method
  • LDMVFI [AAAI2024, code]: Video Frame Interpolation with Latent Diffusion Models
  • MTKD [ECCV2024, code]: Multi-Teacher Knowledge Distillation for Image Super-Resolution
  • HIIF [CVPR2025, code]: Hierarchical Encoding based Implicit Image Function for Continuous Super-resolution
  • BVSR-IK [ICCV2025, code]: Blind Video Super-Resolution based on Implicit Kernels
  • FCVSR [TMM, code]: A frequency-aware Method for Compressed Video Super-Resolution

databases

  • BVI-HD [database]: a perceptual video quality database for HEVC and texture synthesis compressed content
  • BVI-HFR [database]: a high frame rate video database
  • BVI-SR [database]: a study of subjective video quality at various spatial resolutions
  • BVI-HomTex [database]: a video database with various types of homogeneous textures
  • BVI-Texture [database]: a video texture database for perceptual compression and quality assessment
  • BVI-SynTex [database]: a synthetic video texture dataset for video compression and quality assessment
  • BVI-DVC [database]: a training database for deep video compression
  • BVI-CC [database]: a video database for codec comparison and quality assessment
  • BVI-VFI [database]: a subjective quality study for video frame interpolation
  • BVI-UGC [database]: A Video Quality Database for User-Generated Content Transcoding
  • BVI-Artefact [database]: An Artefact Detection Benchmark Dataset for Streamed Videos
  • BVI-AOM [database]: A Training Database for Video Compression Research
  • BVI-CR [database]: A Multi-View Human Dataset for Volumetric Video Compression
  • ViVo [database]: A Dataset for Volumetric VideoReconstruction and Compression
