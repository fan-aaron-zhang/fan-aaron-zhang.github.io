resources

a summary of source code and databases for the works that I have contributed to.

source code

video coding algorithms

ViSTRA [TCSVT2018/SPIC2021]: video compression based on spatial resolution and effective bit depth adaptation
CNN-PP [MM2020]: video compression with CNN-based post processing
MFRNet [JSTSP2020]: a new CNN architecture for post-processing and in-loop filtering
CVE-GAN [SPIC2024]: a perceptually-inspired GAN for compressed video enhancement
HiNeRV [NeurIPS2023]: video compression with hierarchical encoding-based neural representation
MV-HiNeRV [PCS2024]: Immersive Video Compression using Implicit Neural Representations
NVRC [NeurIPS2024]: Neural Video Representation Compression
PNVC [AAAI2025]: a practical INR-based video compression
GIViC [ICCV2025]: generative implicit video compression

quality models

FRQM [ICIP2017]: a frame rate dependent video quality metric
PVM [TCSVT2015]: a perception-based video metric
FloLPIPS [PCS2022]: A Bespoke Video Quality Metric for Frame Interpoation
RankDVQA [WACV2024]: Deep VQA based on a Novel Hybrid Training Methodology
RMT-BVQA [ECCVW2024]: Recurrent Memory Transformer-based Blind Video Quality Assessment for Enhanced Video Content
MVAD [WACV2025]: A Multiple Visual Artifact Detector for Video Streaming

other image processing methods

ST-MFNet [CVPR2022]: Spatio-Temporal Multi-Flow Network for Video Frame Interpolation
ST-MFNet Mini [ICIP2023]: the compact model of ST-MFNet
Deformable Convolution based VFI [ICIP2022]: a new deformable convolution based VFI method
LDMVFI [AAAI2024]: Video Frame Interpolation with Latent Diffusion Models
MTKD [ECCV2024]: Multi-Teacher Knowledge Distillation for Image Super-Resolution
HIIF [CVPR2025]: Hierarchical Encoding based Implicit Image Function for Continuous Super-resolution
BVSR-IK [ICCV2025]: Blind Video Super-Resolution based on Implicit Kernels
FCVSR [TMM]: A frequency-aware Method for Compressed Video Super-Resolution
EfficientSAM3 [arXiv]: Progressive Hierachical Knowledge Distillation (PhD) from SAM1, 2 and 3

databases

BVI-HD: a perceptual video quality database for HEVC and texture synthesis compressed content
BVI-HFR: a high frame rate video database
BVI-SR: a study of subjective video quality at various spatial resolutions
BVI-HomTex: a video database with various types of homogeneous textures
BVI-Texture: a video texture database for perceptual compression and quality assessment
BVI-SynTex: a synthetic video texture dataset for video compression and quality assessment
BVI-DVC: a training database for deep video compression
BVI-CC: a video database for codec comparison and quality assessment
BVI-VFI: a subjective quality study for video frame interpolation
BVI-UGC: A Video Quality Database for User-Generated Content Transcoding
BVI-Artefact: An Artefact Detection Benchmark Dataset for Streamed Videos
BVI-AOM: A Training Database for Video Compression Research
BVI-CR: A Multi-View Human Dataset for Volumetric Video Compression
ViVo: A Dataset for Volumetric VideoReconstruction and Compression
